Bingus Faith Foundation - is an independent collective focused on privacy and personal cybersecurity. Founded by experts in information technology and cybersecurity, BFF advocates for better privacy practices and sets its goal to educate people about how to protect their data in a data-driven economy.

Bingus Faith Foundation Manifesto

  • Personal data is not a commodity

  • Data protection mechanisms should be user-friendly and convenient

  • Personal digital security is as important as physical security

  • Cryptographic mechanisms should be widely-available to the public

  • Fair, privacy-preserving online advertisement
